Future Endeavors

I feel as though my whole life has always been about "what do you want to be in the future?" But even with all that anticipation in my life, I still don't know.
I like to call myself an "all-rounder" for being good at multiple things but it is just a way to make myself feel better about how I have no clue what I want to pursue.
The job I want the most is something that has thrill but I can still enjoy. As of right now I am most interested in being an architect but I think I would also be open to being any type of scientist.
These are my options until I narrow it down. I just want a job that is exciting every single day.
